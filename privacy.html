1. Introduction

Welcome to NeuroLife AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application NeuroLife AI (the "App"), available on iOS and Android.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use NeuroLife AI, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account.
  • User Content: Tasks, calendar events, grocery lists, notes, and voice recordings you create within the App.
  • Communications: Messages you send through the AI chat assistant.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in the App, crash reports.
  • Advertising Identifiers: IDFA (iOS) and GAID (Android) for advertising purposes, subject to your consent.

2.3 Voice Data

If you use voice input features, audio recordings are temporarily processed to convert speech to text. We do not permanently store raw audio recordings after transcription is complete.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features.
  • Process and respond to your requests, tasks, and AI assistant interactions.
  • Send push notifications for task reminders and calendar events (with your permission).
  • Improve and personalize your experience.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App.
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob (with your consent where required).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating the App (e.g., cloud hosting, AI processing). These parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes. See Section 5 for details.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to you.

5. Advertising

NeuroLife AI uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data about you to serve personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage data

iOS Users: We request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your IDFA. You may opt out at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Android Users: We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent for personalized advertising where required by law (e.g., in the EU/EEA under GDPR). You may opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google > Ads.

For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between the App and our servers.
  • Secure storage of authentication tokens using device keychain/keystore.
  • Regular security reviews of our systems.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

NeuroLife AI is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at neurolifeai.app@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising.

To exercise these rights, contact us at neurolifeai.app@gmail.com.

10. iOS-Specific Disclosures

App Tracking Transparency (ATT): On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission before tracking your activity across apps and websites. You may change this permission at any time in your iPhone Settings.

Microphone Access: The App requests microphone access for voice input features. You may revoke this permission in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone.

Notifications: The App requests permission to send push notifications for task and event reminders. You may manage this in Settings > Notifications > NeuroLife AI.

11. Android-Specific Disclosures

Permissions Requested:

  • RECORD_AUDIO — For voice input features.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — For task and event reminders (Android 13+).
  • INTERNET — For AI features and data sync.

Google Play Data Safety: In compliance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, we disclose that we collect device identifiers for advertising purposes and app activity data for analytics. No data is sold to third parties.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by sending a notification through the App. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
